MedaCy Demo

MedaCy is a medical text mining framework built over spaCy to facilitate the engineering, training and application of machine learning models for medical information extraction. This demo allows you to select a model created by the NLP@VCU team and see your results instantly.
  • Clinical Notes is designed to evaluate text you might find in a physician's report for a patient visit.
  • Nanodrug Labels is designed to evaluate text from drug labels, like those that come with prescriptions.
